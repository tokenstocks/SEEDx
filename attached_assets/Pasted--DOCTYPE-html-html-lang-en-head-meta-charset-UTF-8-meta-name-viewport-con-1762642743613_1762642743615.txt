            <p>SEED & SEEDx create a perpetual cycle where capital regenerates, projects thrive, and impact compounds across generations.</p>

            <div class="steps">
                <div class="step">
                    <div class="step-number">1</div>

                <div class="step">
                    <div class="step-number">2</div>
                    <h4>Pools De-Risk Projects</h4>

                <div class="step">
                    <div class="step-number">3</div>
                    <h4>Projects Get Tokenized</h4>
                    <p>Verified projects are tokenized on the blockchain‚Äîcreating tradeable digital assets backed by real farms, solar installations, water systems, and more.</p>
                </div>

                <div class="step">
                    <div class="step-number">4</div>
                    <h4>Regenerators Buy Tokens</h4>
                    <p>Impact investors (Regenerators) purchase tokens, gaining fractional ownership in de-risked projects. Capital flows back to liquidity pools.</p>
                </div>

                <div class="step">
                    <div class="step-number">5</div>
                    <h4>Projects Generate Returns</h4>
                    <p>As projects succeed (harvest sales, energy generation, water subscriptions), returns flow to token holders and liquidity pools.</p>
                </div>

                <div class="step">
                    <div class="step-number">6</div>
                    <h4>Capital Regenerates</h4>
                    <p>Liquidity pools use replenished capital to fund new projects. The cycle repeats perpetually‚Äîsame capital finances 5-10+ projects over time.</p>
